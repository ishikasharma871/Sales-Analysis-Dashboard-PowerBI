#  Dairy Business Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze dairy business performance using sales, inventory, storage, and product-related data.

The dashboard helps identify revenue trends, top-selling products, storage condition impacts, shelf life analysis, and overall business performance through data visualization.

---

##  Business Objectives

- Analyze overall business revenue and product sales.
- Identify top-selling dairy products.
- Study the impact of storage conditions on product quantity.
- Analyze shelf life of dairy products.
- Compare product performance across locations and brands.
- Monitor business KPIs using interactive dashboards.
- Support data-driven business decisions.

##  Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Cows
- Total Land Area

### Interactive Filters
- Location
- Product Name
- Brand
- Sales Channel

### Visualizations

#### Revenue Trend Analysis
Analyzes revenue across Year, Quarter, Month, and Day.

#### Revenue by Location
Compares revenue generated across different locations.

#### Top Selling Products
Displays contribution of products to total sales.

#### Storage Condition Analysis
Shows quantity distribution across storage conditions.

#### Shelf Life Analysis
Analyzes shelf life of dairy products.
